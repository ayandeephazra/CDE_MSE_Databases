Using the first-order Bridgman equation and the Birch equation of state, the isothermal bulk modulus (B0) of nanocrystalline γ-alumina utilizing a diamond anvil cell (DAC) and the energy dispersive X-ray diffraction method was determined to be 162 ± 14 GPa and Young's modulus (E) was estimated to be 253 ± 22 GPa.
